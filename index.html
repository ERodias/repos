Roosty beef

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
beef kneck 1/2 kg in pieces
sweet pepper 2 pieces
salt & pepper 1 small spoon
potatoes 2 pieces
red wine 100 ml

Step 1: Roost the beef pieces and the sweet pepper on fire for 5 minutes.
Step 2: Throw the potatoes in the pan and mix with a woody spoon.
Step 3: Cool down with the red wine for 3 minutes.
Step 4: Throw 150 ml of water and bake in low fire with cover on the pan until the meat will be soft.

Chicken nughets

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
chicken in small pieces 1
sunflower oil 300 ml
salt & pepper 1/2 small spoon

Step 1: Warm up sunflower oil on a deep pan.
Step 2: Fry chicken pieces on high fire for 5 minutes.
Step 3: Dry on kitchen paper for 2 minutes and serve.

Fish fillet

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
fish fillets 4
olive oil 50 ml
salt & pepper 1/2 small spoon

Step 1: Warm up the oven on 200 degrees.
Step 2: Put on a metal oven disk the 4 fillets with olive oil, salt and pepper on top.
Step 3: Bake for 25 minutes on air.
Step 4: Enjoy.
